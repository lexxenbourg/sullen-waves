Sullen Waves is the solo project of Alex Pratsyuk. Alternative and post-grunge: heavy guitars, melodic tension, a dark and introspective core.

Written, recorded, and produced independently, sometimes solo, sometimes with a band. Founded June 2014. Based in Los Angeles.
