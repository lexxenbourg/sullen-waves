Sullen Waves

Alternative · Grunge · Post-rock — Los Angeles

Sullen Waves is the project of Los Angeles musician Alex Pratsyuk. Formed in 2014 and now a solo undertaking, it takes its name from two things held together — sullen for mood, waves for sound frequency. Pratsyuk writes music first and lets vocals and lyrics follow, a method that runs from the 2015 single “Seven” and the Sense of Emergency EP through “King of Loneliness” (2021) and the 2025 single “Experience from Before” — a song he carried unfinished for years and released without smoothing its original edges. The new single “Echoes from the Void” arrives June 26, 2026.
